Our Mission

We are on a mission to eliminate physical queues and give people their time back.

The Problem

Waiting in line is one of the most inefficient uses of human time. Whether it's at a restaurant, a clinic, or a DMV, physical queues are stressful, crowded, and outdated.

We believe technology should serve people, not the other way around. ZeroWait was born from a simple question: "Why do we still stand in line in the 21st century?"

⏳ ➡️ ☕

From waiting to living.

The Solution

ZeroWait provides a digital bridge between businesses and customers. By moving the queue to the cloud, we allow customers to wait wherever they feel most comfortable—at a cafe, in their car, or at home.

For businesses, we provide the tools to manage customer flow, reduce crowding, and increase efficiency, all while providing a superior customer experience.
